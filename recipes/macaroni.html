Macaroni

This delightful classic macaroni salad is a staple that always garners praise and is a favorite at gatherings, being an ideal dish for picnics, potlucks, and family events. The recipe is straightforward, making it easy to prepare while delivering a taste that consistently wins over everyone who tries it. Its creamy texture, combined with a sweet and tangy flavor profile, creates a comforting and satisfying experience, perfect for any occasion.

The salad is a colorful and appealing mix, featuring elbow macaroni as the base, complemented by a variety of vegetables and a creamy dressing. The dressing, a blend of mayonnaise, yellow mustard, sugar, and vinegar, offers a perfect balance of flavors, while the inclusion of celery, onion, bell pepper, and optional carrots and pimentos adds a delightful crunch and visual appeal. This dish not only satisfies the palate but also provides a visually striking addition to any meal table.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ⅔ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup distilled white vinegar
  • 2 ½ tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni in boiling, lightly salted water until tender yet firm, then rinse under cold water and drain.
  2. In a large bowl, mix mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper, then add the macaroni, stirring well.
  3. Incorporate the celery, onion, green pepper, carrot, and pimentos into the mix.
  4. Chill the salad in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld.
