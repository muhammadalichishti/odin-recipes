Better Baked Beans

Better Baked Beans offer a nostalgic twist on a classic side dish, embodying the essence of comfort food with a semi-homemade approach that's perfect for gatherings or a family dinner. The dish starts with canned baked beans, simplifying the preparation while ensuring a rich and satisfying base. The addition of chopped onion introduces a subtle sharpness, which, when melded with the sweetness of pancake syrup and brown sugar, creates a delightful contrast. Ketchup and yellow mustard add tangy notes, rounding out the flavor profile of the beans, making them a versatile accompaniment to various main courses.

The preparation process is straightforward, ideal for cooks of any skill level. After mixing the beans with the onion, syrup, brown sugar, ketchup, and mustard, the mixture is transferred to a baking dish. Topped with bacon slices, the dish is then baked until the bacon is crisped to perfection, and the beans have thickened, achieving a richly flavored and visually appealing dish. This method not only infuses the beans with a smoky bacon essence but also adds a delightful texture contrast, making the dish a comforting and hearty option for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 (28 ounce) cans baked beans
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons pancake syrup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons prepared yellow mustard
  • 4 slices bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the baked beans, onion, syrup, brown sugar, ketchup, and mustard.
  3. Pour the mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Lay the bacon slices across the top.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, until the bacon is browned and the beans have thickened.
